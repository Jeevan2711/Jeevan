Mother Theresa

Mother Theresa Institute of Engineering and Technology, a premier Engineering College, was established in the year 2010 with the objective of developing competent and responsible technocrats for the futuristic needs of India. Promoted by "Holy Cross Educational Society, Palamaner".

What is the highest package offered at Mother Teresa College Palamaner?
The highest salary package offered is 7.5 LPA. The average salary package offered is 3 LPA. Nearly 90% of students are placed.
